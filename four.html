UK Medical School Selector

We created this tool to be used as a guide to assist with decisions for medical school applications. We appreciate that the scoring and admissions criteria for medical school is complex and it is not possible for this tool to take into account all the factors considered by universities when scoring applications. We would therefore not recommened that individuals apply solely based on the information from this tool and advise individuals to combine the information obtained from this tool with information from other sources. We hope that this tool will assist you with decisions on university courses.


All the best with your applications!
